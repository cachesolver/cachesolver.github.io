Cache Solver - About

We always struggled with pieces of paper while caching, or little notes on our phone. Or even sending the codes we found as a message to the other cachers that were joining us on our hunt. And then by the time we had all the details, the calculations started, and we made errors and we logged DNF's because of it.

Then we started using Google Sheets with pre filled formulas , so while walking it was just a matter of filling in the sheet and the result came out . It was better But writing formulas in Sheets style was still painfull. And difficult to share with others.

Now what if we could just build an app that did all of this for us. So the idea for Cache Solver was born

Fill in the Parameters, Fill in the forumula , share with your friends and go outside. A static one page open source application

You can save and import formula for the caches you are searching, on your phone, on your laptop and share them with your friends or other devices.

We don't store anything, we don't know who you are or where you are caching, unless you mail your formulas to us.
